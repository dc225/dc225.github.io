DC225

Welcome to the DEF CON 225 website. DEF CON 225 (DC225) is a local DEF CON group for the 225 area code and surrounding areas. 337, 318, 225, 504 area codes are represented.

DC225 is composed of students, professionals, researchers, and hobbyists that meet and discuss current trends, topics, and research in the fields of computer security, digital forensics, and related.
