DC225

Welcome to the DEF CON 225 website. DEF CON 225 (DC225) is a local DEF CON group for the 225 area code and surrounding areas. 337, 318, 225, 504, and 985 area codes are represented.

DC225 is composed of students, professionals, researchers, and hobbyists that meet and discuss current trends, topics, and research in the fields of computer security, digital forensics, and related.

If you are into hacking, OSINT, nerdy stuffs, threat intel, or cybersecurity, we are the group for you. Join us!

We support all our hacker frens and all are welcome. We follow the educational motto of "Each one, teach one". If you dont know how to do something and an other member does, we all learn from and teach each other.
